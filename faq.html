Covid-19 Updates:

Due to current circumstances, we are experiencing higher demands so please give us a 48 hour grace period.

How quickly does my order ship to my house?

Our staff hand picks your ingredients within 24 hours of your purchase. We than ship immediately which takes 5-7 business days.

Where do we get our produce from?

We support local businesses and farms! We are lucky to be in California where there is lots of farmers markets and fresh produce.

Where do you deliver?

Currently we are a small start up so we only deliver within California.

Can I customize my order?

Yes, you can choose however many or however few meal options with the same delivery fee of $10.

Why is there a delivery fee?

We believe in paying our employees fairly so we have to charge for delivery.

How many meals should I order?

Each meal is made for 2 people. So if you want to cook for 4 people, than order two of the same meal.
