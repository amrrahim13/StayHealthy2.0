Our Mission:

Deliver ingredients for meals so that you can focus on what you want to eat and not shopping, and last minute errands runs...all this from an app.


Our Motivation:

I started this company for myself actually. I was very confused about what to buy in a store. If I wanted to make spaghetti for dinner, what ingredients did I need. I would get some ingredients and go home and realize I forgot the pasta sauce. So StayHealthy is about having complete meal packages that conveniently include everything you need and deliver it to you so you can focus on cooking instead of shopping. That way you have more time with family at dinner time and can use your free time for your hobbies.
